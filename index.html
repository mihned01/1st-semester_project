The plastic reality

The problem:

Every year, over 11 million tons of plastic enter our oceans, polluting marine ecosystems and threatening wildlife. Seals, like many marine animals, suffer from this crisis often becoming entangled in plastic waste or mistaking it for food, which can lead to starvation or death. Shockingly, traces of microplastics have even been found in fish, whales but also in human blood and mother’s milk, showing the far-reaching effects of this pollution.

Take action:

The fight against plastic pollution starts with awareness and action. You can be part of the solution by reducing single-use plastics, supporting the Global Plastic Treaty, and educating others about the far-reaching effects of this crisis.

Our ambition for a planet free of plastic

At Our Planet Free of Plastic, we believe in a world where plastic is no longer a threat to our environment. Our mission is to be a part of the solution, not just a passive bystander. That’s why we’re supporting the Global Plastic Treaty — an initiative that will bring real change to the way the world handles plastic pollution.

Why the Global Plastic Treaty matters

The Global Plastic Treaty is a landmark agreement that aims to significantly reduce plastic waste worldwide. By supporting it, we are advocating for policies that regulate the production, use, and disposal of plastic, ensuring a cleaner, healthier planet for future generations.

Our commitment

Our brand is dedicated to eliminating plastic from our products, packaging, and processes. But we know that individual efforts aren't enough. We need global change, and the Global Plastic Treaty is the key to driving that transformation. By supporting this treaty, we’re amplifying our voice for the planet.

The power of collective action

Each person who supports the Global Plastic Treaty adds to a growing movement calling for action. Together, we have the power to influence governments and corporations, making them accountable for their role in the plastic crisis. When we all stand together, we can turn the tide on plastic pollution.

Why your support matters

Signing the Global Plastic Treaty isn't just a symbolic act. It’s a direct contribution to a global movement that will result in stronger regulations, cleaner oceans, and healthier ecosystems. By joining us, you are part of a collective effort that’s shaping a future free of plastic waste.

Real Impact, Real Change

With over 3 million voices already rallying behind the Global Plastic Treaty, we’ve seen positive changes in policy and regulations aimed at reducing plastic pollution. The treaty represents an opportunity to hold companies accountable for their plastic footprint. By signing, you're showing that you care about the future of our oceans, wildlife, and planet.
